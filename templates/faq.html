FAQs



How does Study Planner create my schedule?

Study Planner analyzes your available time and your scores for each subject, then uses a weighted algorithm to distribute your study time in a balanced and goal-oriented way.


Do I need to manually set priorities for my lessons?

No. Priorities are calculated automatically based on your performance, so you don't need to guess which subject is more important.


Why do some subjects get more study time than others?

Subjects with lower scores require more attention. The system allocates more time to areas where improvement is needed most.


Can any subject receive zero study time?

No. Every subject always receives a minimum amount of study time to ensure balanced learning.


What happens if I have very limited free time?

The planner still distributes your time fairly by maintaining proportional allocation and minimum study durations.


Can I change my plan later?

Yes. You can regenerate your study plan at any time by updating your scores or available time.


Is my data stored permanently?

Currently, your data is only used to generate your study plan. Future versions may include optional data storage for long-term tracking.


Who is this application designed for?

Study Planner is designed for students who know what they need to study but struggle with time management and prioritization.


Is Study Planner suitable for all educational levels?

Yes. The algorithm is flexible and works for different subjects, scoring systems, and study loads.
